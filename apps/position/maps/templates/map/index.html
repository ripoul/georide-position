Georide road trip ! Why ?

Georide allow to share a trip only when we have already finish it. My application allow the georide user to share his position for the time he need. If the time is not finish, we can follow his position live.

You want to share your road trip ?

Step 0 : You need a georide account !

You can check their website

Step 1 : Create account

Create an account, fill all the informations requiered. You can have help on create account page with the information about georide.

Sign Up
Step 2 : share the url to everyone

You can share this url : https://georide.ripoul.fr/trip/your-username. With this url, your friend can follow you live and have some information.

That's it !

The user can select only the dates in your road trip.
